Brief information about the Vidyalaya and the alumni association.

The Navodaya Vidyalaya System is a unique experiment unparalleled in the annals of school education in India whose significance lies in the selection of talented rural children as the target group and the attempt to provide them with quality education comparable to the best in a residential school system. Navodaya Vidyalayas are there in every districts of all Indian states and union territories except Tamilnadu.

Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalaya, Tarbha is the Vidyalaya for Subarnapur district of the state Odisha. The Vidyalaya started its operation in 2001 and it's first batch completed their Tenth standard in 2006 and migrated to other Vidyalayas as being in the temporary site, the Vidyalaya had no accommodation for Intermediate studies. The Vidyalaya is now moved to its permanent site and Intermediate studies for Arts and Science streams have been introduced.

This website is dedicated to all the alumni studied in the Vidyalaya. This website has been developed and maintained by the alumni themselves. There are many volunteer alumni who are associated with the association in many different positions leading the association from the front.
